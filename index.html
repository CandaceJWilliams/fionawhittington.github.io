About

Shehacks is an endeavor on behalf of women from universities all around Boston, including Wellesley, Tufts, MIT, UMASS Boston, BC and more to host the world's largest all-female and femme nonbinary hackathon.

The mission of sheBOS is to: empower women and femme nonbinary individuals in computer science to achieve; provide them with opportunities to explore the tech industry in an inspiring, encouraging, and energizing environment; and to create a community of inclusivity within the Boston tech industry.
